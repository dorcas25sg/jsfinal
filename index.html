Predictive Modelling: Bike Share in Seattle

Today, Seattle's Proto Bike Share is no longer in operation. One of its serious issues was in rebalancing bikes between stations with insufficient bikes and those with riders waiting for an empty slot to park their bike. In this study, my teammate, Meiqing, and I proposed a possible app (not built) to help in the rebalancing process through predictive modelling to guide and plan the rebalancing process.
